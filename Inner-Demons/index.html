How To play

How to Play:

Movement Controls

To move around, the player uses the [A] and [D] keys. (A for left, and D for right)
The way the game is styled restricts upwards and downwards movement. You may only go left and right.

WARNING Do not use the arrow keys AT ALL while you have the games window selected.
Doing so can cause the game to crash, and none of us want that.

Loot

In the game, the player has a chance to come across chests.
If the player walks in front of the chest, they are able to open the chest.
So long as the player is standing in front of the chest, they can press the [E] key to open
the chest. Doing so isn't always going to be a good thing, so it's risky.

If you do happen to get loot, then you can check the "Pause" menu which is literally just an inventory.
In this menu, you can change which weapon you have equipped.

Please note: The "Pause" button doesn't actually pause anything. If you pause while being attacked,
the enemy will not stop for you to change to your glowing spear, this ain't fallout.

Attacking

This one is fairly straight forward... just walk up to the enemy and click,
then it should be dead. (It's not actually THAT easy)

About the Game

About The Game:

This is a dungeon crawler game where the player gets multiple customization choices.
They are able to choose their gender, and one of the preset character looks, based on their gender.
They are able to collect cool items, and they can fight monsters down in the deepest depths of a dungeon.
The player has access to an inventory menu, where they can swap out their weapons, and experiment with the
damage modifiers given by each weapon.
The player is able to gain these weapons from searching chests and gaining loot.

BUT BE WARY:
The chests don't always give you helpful boosts...

The player can fight a giant boss monster, and truly face their Inner Demons.
